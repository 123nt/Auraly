Our Commitment to Privacy

At Auraly, we believe in absolute privacy. We don't collect any personal information, period. Our app is designed for music lovers who want to enjoy their music without worrying about data collection or tracking.

Zero Data Collection

We want to be crystal clear:

  • We don't collect any personal information

Just Music, Nothing Else

Auraly is all about the music. We focus on providing the best music experience possible without any unnecessary data collection. Your privacy is not just a priority - it's our foundation.
